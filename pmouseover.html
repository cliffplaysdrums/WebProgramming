DESCRIPTION:

This is an attempt to simplify the concept of a rocket generating thrust. It is a classical example of Newtonian mechanics. However, this same concept can be applied to engines that rely on ion propulsion. Essentially the gas is given a magnetic charge and is driven out of the nozzle via a magnet rather than combustion. We'll see an example in the VASIMR engine.
